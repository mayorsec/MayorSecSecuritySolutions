About MayorSec

MayorSec was founded in 2017 by Aaron Adams and Jonathon Taylor after the local power plant they were working at was ransomware attacked. Aaron and Jonathon realized that the industry lacked a signifcant apparatus for proactively approaching cyber security and ensuring threats are minimized before they become liabilities.

MayorSec has since grown to include multiple penetration testers and subject matter experts in the field, each with a keen understanding and attention to detail in all things cyber security.

Services

MayorSec is a one-stop shop for offensive cybersecurity operations. Our organization conducts penetration testing, phishing, vishing, red teaming, phyiscal assessments, AWS and Azure assessments, and more.
